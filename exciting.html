Time Off

I do have like a week between when school ends and that CSM class starts. Think I'll spend a little of that time getting a jump start, so I'm not completely lost the first day. Another thing I'm trying to do is finish the complete works of Nietzsche, which isn't as big of a task as it sounds. He was a writer of many books, but most aren't especially technical; not an encyclopedic philosopher such as Hegel, Kant, and Aristotle. Now if you want to read and understand those three, you're in for a ride. It's also a little easier for me since I've read other philosophical works. The Kaufman translations are basically the universally accepted English translations. You can get his main works in two compact volume. He also wrote a biography of him, so that's a good primer.
